Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Campus Food Ordering Platform.

Information We Collect

  • User account information from the authentication provider
  • Order history and transaction information
  • Vendor menu data and product details
  • Dietary and allergen preferences where applicable

How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Process and manage food orders
  • Provide real-time order tracking
  • Improve vendor menu management
  • Generate analytics such as peak ordering hours

Third-Party Services

Authentication and payment processing are handled through trusted third-party providers. These services have their own privacy policies governing how data is handled.

Data Protection

Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are implemented to protect user data against unauthorised access or misuse.

Student Data and Dietary Information

Menu items may include dietary and allergen information based on publicly available data sources to assist students with dietary requirements such as halal, vegan, or nut-free options.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as the system evolves or regulatory requirements change.

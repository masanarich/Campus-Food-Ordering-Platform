Purpose of the Platform

This platform allows university students to browse menus from multiple vendors, place food orders online, and collect meals without waiting in long queues. Vendors can manage menus and orders, while administrators oversee vendor accounts and platform operations.

User Accounts

Users must authenticate using a third-party identity provider. Three roles exist on the platform:

  • Students (Customers) who place orders
  • Vendors who manage menus and orders
  • Administrators who manage vendor accounts

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their accounts and for any activity conducted through them.

Ordering and Payments

Students may place orders from participating campus vendors. Payments are processed through a third-party payment gateway. The platform itself does not store payment details.

Vendor Responsibilities

Vendors are responsible for ensuring menu accuracy, food availability, pricing correctness, and preparation of orders within reasonable timeframes.

Limitation of Liability

The platform acts as an intermediary between students and vendors. Responsibility for food preparation, safety, and quality remains with the individual vendors.

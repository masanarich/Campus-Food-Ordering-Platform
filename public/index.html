About the Platform

Built to reduce queues and improve the campus food experience

The Campus Food Ordering Platform is a web-based system that allows students to order food online, vendors to manage menus and incoming orders, and administrators to monitor platform activity across the system.

Core Features

What the platform offers

Multi-vendor Ordering

Students can browse different vendors and place orders easily from one platform.

Real-Time Tracking

Follow order progress from received to preparing to ready for pickup.

Menu Management

Vendors can add items, update prices, and mark items as sold out.

Analytics

Admins and vendors can view reports and useful ordering trends.
